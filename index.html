Role-Based Access

Built for the Entire Care Team

Every role has a tailored experience designed to maximize efficiency and response times.

Administrator

  • Approve staff & doctor accounts
  • Manage resident profiles
  • Monitor facility operations
  • Broadcast system announcements

Medical Team

  • Review resident medical profiles
  • Track clinic appointments
  • Prescribe & monitor medicines
  • Provide urgent updates

Care Staff / Nurse

  • Log daily vitals & routines
  • Check off medication timings
  • View scheduled duties
  • Report immediate emergencies

Receptionist

  • Register incoming residents
  • Manage appointment calendars
  • Handle visitor logs
  • Send notifications to doctors
Features Overview

Everything You Need to Run Comfortably

Serenity handles all logistics from resident registration to emergency health alerts.

Detailed Resident Directory

Maintain detailed profile histories, emergency contacts, room assignments, and doctor notes in one unified cloud database (Firebase Firestore).

Appointment Scheduling

Receptionists can book checkups directly with doctors. Doctors receive live notification cards and schedule updates on their dashboards.

Medication Logs

Ensure prescriptions are administered precisely. Staff and nurses can view schedule timings and update completed dosages in real time.

FCM Push Notifications

Powered by Firebase Cloud Messaging. Instantly alert specific doctors of emergency updates or upcoming checkups, keeping response times under minutes.
